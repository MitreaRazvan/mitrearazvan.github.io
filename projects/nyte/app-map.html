Navigation Model
Between Tabs
Digital Crown
Scroll up/down to switch between the 5 main tabs. Vertical slide transition, 300ms.
Into Detail
Tap → Push Right
Tap a friend, button, or card to push deeper. 350ms smart animate. Back via swipe or chevron.
Alerts
Dissolve Overlay
Poke, hydration, milestone, song — appear as dissolve overlays. 250ms in, 200ms out.
Emergency
Hard Cut
Instant transition. 0ms. No animation. Urgency over elegance.
